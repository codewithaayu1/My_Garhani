🏡 Welcome to Garhani Village

Garhani is a historic and culturally rich village located in the Bhojpur district of Bihar, India. Known for its fertile farmlands, deep-rooted traditions, and warm community spirit — this village represents the true heart of rural Bihar. Here you will experience the peace of open fields, the sound of birds at dawn, and the warmth of a close-knit community that has thrived for generations.
